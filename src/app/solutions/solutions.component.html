DTV Proof of Concept

Just recently, one of the Flex Force Innovation Squads worked with DTV on a Proof of Concept (PoC) for a scoring tool. The Work Flow Engine Coaching and Scoring Tool DTV POC delivered data insights to managers, increasing care agent conformance with the Work Flow Engine (WFE). The targeted coaching that the trial enabled resulted in a 4% decrease in dispatches, 1% reduction in repeat calls, and lowered average call handle times by over 1 minute. The application, if implemented, is estimated to realize $30M in savings in its first full year.
